Types of Chefs

    The head chef of a professional kitchen or of a group of professional kitchens.

    Oversees the production of pastries, sweets, breads, ice creams, toppings and fillings.

  • Chef de Cuisine
    Oversees, executes and develops menus for Restaurants
  • Exec. Sous Chef/Sous Chef
    Second in authority in a restaurant or kitchen, ranking below the chef de cuisine
